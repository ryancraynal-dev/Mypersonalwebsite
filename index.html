Who I Am

I’m someone who is focused on growth in every area of life. I train consistently, push myself in rowing, and stay disciplined even when it’s difficult. I’m building both physical strength and mental resilience.

I’m also deeply interested in business and investing. I spend time learning how money works, how companies grow, and how to think long-term. My goal is to build a life where I have control, independence, and the ability to create something meaningful.

Everything I do is centered around improving — whether that’s in the gym, on the water, in school, or in learning new skills. I’m focused on becoming the best version of myself.
